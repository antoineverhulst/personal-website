# About Me

**Data and Product Expert** | Data Product Owner at BlaBlaCar

## Profile

I am an expert in data strategy, combining technical expertise with strategic vision to design data and AI products in the transportation sector. My goal is to apply this dual competency to regulation and the design of more effective and equitable public policies. As a data leader, I guide teams to define strategies aligned with business objectives to facilitate decision-making and increase user autonomy and impact. My dual background in business engineering and econometrics research allows me to bridge technical challenges and socio-economic objectives.
