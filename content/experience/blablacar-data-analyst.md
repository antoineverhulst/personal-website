## Data Analyst

Spent 5 years building data infrastructure and analytics capabilities for BlaBlaCar Daily, focusing on supporting public policy, product development, and fraud prevention in the daily carpooling space.

### Database Management & Infrastructure

- Managed databases for the BlaBlaCar Daily application
- Integrated two different databases following Klaxit app acquisition
- Built and maintained data pipelines for real-time and batch processing

### Analytics for Public Policy

#### Automated Dashboards for Mobility Authorities
- Designed and deployed automated dashboards for Mobility Organizing Authorities (AOM) and Regions
- Enabled precise tracking of carpooling subsidy policy effectiveness
- Provided data-driven insights to inform public transportation policy decisions

### Product & Strategic Analytics

- Conducted user data analysis to inform:
  - Product development decisions
  - Strategic planning initiatives
  - Marketing campaigns
  - Territorial expansion strategies

### Technical Project Management

Led multiple data and technical projects including:

- **Pricing Grid Deployment:** Implemented new pricing algorithms and tracked their performance
- **Connection Process Tracking:** Monitored user onboarding and engagement flows
- **Database Integration:** Successfully merged Klaxit databases post-acquisition
- **Fraud Detection:** Implemented automated tools and Data Science models for fraud prevention
- **Search Optimization:** Developed models for improving carpooling match quality

### Impact

- Processed and analyzed data from millions of carpooling trips
- Directly supported public policy decisions through data transparency
- Improved product experience through data-driven iteration
- Reduced fraud through automated detection systems

### Skills Applied

SQL, Python, BigQuery, Looker Studio, Tableau, Data Warehousing, ETL Development, Machine Learning, Fraud Detection, Public Policy Analysis
