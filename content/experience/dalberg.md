## Data Analyst Intern

Worked on geospatial data analysis and visualization projects for public transportation and urban mobility in developing countries.

### Key Projects

#### Public Transport Network Development
- Created interactive dashboards using Tableau
- Performed data mapping using QGIS and Mapbox
- Processed mobile phone datasets to generate usable mobility data
- Delivered reports to Transport ministries and development bank officials

#### Paris Autolib Analysis
- Analyzed electric car sharing charging station data for Paris city hall
- Evaluated usage patterns and infrastructure optimization opportunities
- Provided recommendations for electric vehicle infrastructure planning

### Technical Skills Developed

- **GIS:** QGIS, Mapbox for geospatial visualization
- **Business Intelligence:** Tableau for interactive dashboards
- **Big Data:** Processing large-scale mobile phone datasets
- **Client Communication:** Presenting to government officials and development banks

### Impact
