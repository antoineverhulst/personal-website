# DecodeTech: Technology & Society

## Information Blog on Technology's Impact on Society

Co-founder and author of **DecodeTech**, a blog dedicated to exploring how technology shapes our cities, societies, and daily lives through insights from practitioners and experts.

### Mission

DecodeTech aims to bridge the gap between technological innovation and its societal implications, providing accessible analysis based on real-world experience and expert perspectives.

### Content Focus

- **Urban Technology:** How digital tools reshape cities and public spaces
- **Artificial Intelligence:** Practical applications and societal implications
- **Mobility & Transportation:** The future of urban movement
- **Data & Privacy:** Balancing innovation with individual rights
- **Public Policy:** Technology's role in governance and regulation

### Featured Articles

#### "Artificial Intelligence and the City - A Parking Story"

This article examines how AI is being applied to urban parking management, exploring both the technical solutions and their broader implications for city planning, equity, and urban life. Through a concrete case study, the article demonstrates how seemingly simple technological applications can have complex societal effects.

**Key themes:**
- Smart city technologies
- Data-driven urban planning
- Equity in technology deployment
- Public vs. private interests in urban tech

### Approach

DecodeTech distinguishes itself by:
- **Practitioner Insights:** Drawing from real-world implementation experience
- **Accessible Analysis:** Making complex topics understandable for general audiences
- **Critical Perspective:** Examining both benefits and challenges of technological change
- **Cross-Disciplinary:** Connecting technology, economics, urban planning, and social sciences

### Audience

The blog serves policymakers, urban planners, technology professionals, researchers, and engaged citizens interested in understanding how technology shapes our collective future.

### Impact

By providing informed, accessible analysis, DecodeTech contributes to more thoughtful public discourse about technology policy and helps bridge the knowledge gap between technical experts and decision-makers.
