## Research Master in Economics

Completed a specialized research master at Université Libre de Bruxelles with a focus on Transportation and Environmental Economics in collaboration with KU Leuven.

### Specialization

**Transportation and Environmental Economics** (KU Leuven partnership)

This program provided advanced training in:
- Economic analysis of transportation systems
- Environmental economics and policy
- Econometric methods for policy evaluation
- Urban economics and mobility
- Sustainable transportation systems

### Research Focus

The program emphasized:
- Quantitative research methods
- Economic modeling of transportation networks
- Policy analysis and evaluation
- Statistical and econometric techniques
- Environmental impact assessment

### Skills Developed

- Advanced econometrics and statistical analysis
- Transportation systems analysis
- Environmental policy evaluation
- Research methodology
- Economic modeling

This foundation has been instrumental in my career analyzing carpooling and urban mobility data, enabling me to bridge economic theory with practical transportation policy.
