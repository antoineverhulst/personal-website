## Bachelor in Business Engineering

Completed undergraduate studies in Business Engineering at Université Catholique de Louvain, providing a comprehensive foundation in business, engineering, and analytical thinking.

### Program Overview

The three-year program combined:
- Engineering fundamentals
- Business and management principles
- Mathematics and statistics
- Economics and finance
- Computer science and information systems

### Foundation Skills

Developed core competencies in:
- Analytical problem-solving
- Mathematical and statistical methods
- Business fundamentals
- Engineering thinking
- Quantitative analysis

This foundation set the stage for advanced studies in quantitative methods and transportation economics.
